Critical Design Framework Assessment
Sensory Design, Autonomy & Inclusion  ·  Desk-based instrument  ·  Three case studies
Purpose. This instrument evaluates six dimensions central to the dissertation argument: framework evidence quality, sensory autonomy, greenhouse effect risk, inclusion versus enclave architecture, neuroinclusive breadth, and participatory design. Scores are derived from published evidence only. All notes are editable.
Profiles reveal design philosophy rather than performance rankings. Analytical notes beneath each dimension are editable and saved with your file.
